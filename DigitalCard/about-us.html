Our Story

Founded in 2001 during South Africa's HIV/AIDS crisis, Right to Care pioneered life-saving treatment for public sector patients. Today, we are global leaders in healthcare access, donor fund management, and patient-centered innovation. With support from international partners, we continue to redefine public health systems and empower communities worldwide.

  • Health Systems Strengthening
  • Digital Health & Data Analytics
  • Community Outreach & Education
  • Global Health Partnerships
